Data Practices

Data Collection

  • Contact Information: Email address, full name, phone number (optional)
  • Financial Data: Transactions, accounts, budgets, savings goals, bill splitting data
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, IP address
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, actions performed, crash reports
  • Authentication: Passwords (encrypted), MFA secrets (encrypted), biometric data

Data Usage

  • Providing and improving the Application
  • Authentication and security
  • Fraud detection and prevention
  • Customer support and technical assistance
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Legal and regulatory compliance

Tracking & Identifiers

NO TRACKING

Finmate does NOT engage in tracking as defined by Apple's privacy standards. We do not use advertising identifiers, IDFA, or other identifiers for cross-app or cross-website tracking.

Third-Party Services

Service Providers

  • Supabase: Cloud database and authentication services
  • Sentry: Error tracking and crash reporting
  • Apple Services: Authentication, push notifications

Data Sharing

We only share personal data with third-party service providers necessary to operate the Application. All third parties are contractually obligated to maintain data confidentiality and security.

Data Security

  • Encryption in Transit: TLS 1.3 / HTTPS for all connections
  • Encryption at Rest: AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
  • Authentication: bcrypt password hashing, JWT tokens, biometric support
  • Database: Row-level security policies, encrypted storage
  • Access Control: Role-based access control, limited employee access

User Rights & Controls

  • Data Access: Users can export their data in JSON/CSV format
  • Data Deletion: Users can permanently delete their account and all associated data
  • Preferences: Users control notification, theme, and privacy settings
  • Consent: Clear consent required during signup and for new features

Data Retention

  • Active Users: Data retained while account is active
  • Account Deletion: Personal data deleted within 30 days of deletion request
  • Transaction History: May be retained for 7 years for legal/tax purposes
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days for analysis

Regulatory Compliance

GDPR (EU/UK Users)

Legal basis for processing: Contract, Consent, Legal Obligation, Legitimate Interests. Users have rights to access, rectification, erasure, and data portability.

CCPA (California Users)

Users have rights to know what data is collected, delete their information, and opt-out of any potential sales/sharing.

COPPA (Children's Privacy)

Finmate is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
